How The Last of Us became a cult game, and its sequel became a scandal

The game is dedicated to the very last of us, people who survived the apocalypse. In my opinion, this is one of its advantages. She speaks to the player on topics that will always be relevant. What is man and humanity? What is life and what is death? Is Mankind really the crown of creation? Where does good begin, and where does evil begin, is it possible to divide these concepts at all? The list is incomplete. There is an opportunity to extend it, but I think the idea is clear: one of the "pillars" of the plot is reflections on the concept of Humanity and man.

More human than people. Detroit: Become Human

The entire filming process is divided into several stages. First, the body, voice, and facial expressions of each actor are captured. This stage took Quantic Dream about 100 days. The other 250 days were spent filming all the action scenes and other character movements. Based on the material received, the developers prepared about 37,000 animations. As a result, about three hundred actors were involved in the filming, and two hundred more were working on the game itself.
